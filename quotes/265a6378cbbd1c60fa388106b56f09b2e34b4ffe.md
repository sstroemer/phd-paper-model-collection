_Comment on an intuitive relation between Benders decomposition and SDDP._

## Quote(s)

> SDDP for single scenario problem is just nested Benders. So it is deterministic in your case. However, for more general problems (multiple-scenarios ones), it is stochastic, since it involves sampling.
